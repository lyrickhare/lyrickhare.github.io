About Me

Lyric Khare

Hello there! Welcome to my personal website. I'm Lyric Khare, an enthusiastic individual currently in my 4th year of B.tech in Aerospace Engineering at IIT Bombay. With a diverse background, I've had the opportunity to work in the exciting fields of Fintech, Insurance, and Robotics. As a technology and math enthusiast, I constantly seek new challenges and thrive in finding innovative solutions. Recently, finance has become a high-priority interest of mine. When I'm not busy with my studies or work, you can find me cycling alongside serene lakes or in the midst of nature. I also enjoy playing chess, although I must admit I'm not the best at it. Throughout my journey, I've worked on various projects, including building and deploying ML models on the cloud, analyzing loan disbursal for sales and operations, designing electrical architecture for underwater vehicles, and even exploring my creative side through voice-over and content writing. I'm excited to share more about my experiences and connect with like-minded individuals. Let's embark on this journey together!
